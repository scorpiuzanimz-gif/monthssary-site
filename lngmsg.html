First Month ❤️

1 month ago, March 13th 2026, that day, a day I vividly remember. I remember how worried I was on what I would tell to you. How much I hesitated to see you. And how scared I was to say what I felt, thinking it wouldn't be enough. I scrambled my mind thinking; what do I say? How can I make this special? A ball of noodles I was.

When we first became a thing, I didn't expect much. But I had one single thought I set in stone; it will end, so don't get too attached. And for the first week, I did hold onto that, maybe too much. To the point that when you kept talking about the future, one I was in, I felt guilty in a way because I didn't want to hurt you. You seemed so sure, and I was here still thinking.

But then I read that letter again, and suddenly; I felt flashbacks, to the times we spent, the laughs we shared, and the moments of just listening to eachother ramble about our interests. And suddenly, I now knew why I loved you. I knew I loved you, but I didn't know why. But when I did, when I realized why you matter so much to me, it made me love you even more. It made me want to look forward to the future, one where you're in. And I want to work towards that goal, with you by my side.

Happy 1st Monthsary, Sybelle!! First Milestone out of many. First of many I look forward to. And first of many that I want to experience with you. Thank you for so much, so much that I can't even write them all down or else this would be too long, so much that I go to bed at night still unable to understand how I met such an amazing person and how that person loves me, how that person loves me for who I am.

I don't say it enough, or maybe I say it too much. But I appreciate everything you do for me, whether it's just the jokes, the encouragement, the reassurances, the lessons you taught me, the memories we made. They're ones I wouldn't sacrifice for the world. You're amazing to say the least, you're funny, you're smart, you're something words can't describe.

This first month was a memorable one, it had its ups and downs, and in those it helped us understand eachother much deeper. This first month, you made me realize how much I love you and how I didn't need to be a different person just to be loved. You are my yellow, a person who brings joy and warmth to my life, a safe space that made me feel like myself once again. Thank you, truly thank you for believing in me, for lighting up the days others could not, for giving me the confidence to be myself, and for loving me for who I am. I cannot thank you enough.

I want to keep moving forward with this, I want us to keep moving forward, to see eachothers growth, to see eachother accomplish our dreams, and to see what the future may hold. Together. The present and the past hasn't been the smoothest of roads, but maybe it's why I love it, because even in those bumps, we kept persevering, that even in the hurdles, it didn't break us, and that in the end we still chose eachother

I originally wanted to read this to you, bouquet in hand, you by my side, the weight of your hand on mine. But circumstance has made that difficult to say the least, so this is the best of my ability for now. I hate the fact that I cannot love you openly, that I can't be with you due to it, that at best; I'm only able to love you through a screen, that I cannot just hold you like I want to.

But even after all of this, even if I can't be with you now, even if I can't hug you tightly anymore, even if I can't simply just sit down with you in the same place, just being two idiots who love eachother, I'll still choose you. If having to pretend to not love you now so somewhere in the future I can love you openly then I'm willing to take it. If loving you means challenges, hurdles, and stipulations, I would still choose you over and over.

I love you Sybelle, I love you always. And I wouldn't trade you or what we have for the world.
